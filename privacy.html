Information we may collect

We may collect information you choose to provide, such as your name, email address, phone number, business information, project details, scheduling information, uploaded documents, and messages sent to the studio.

When you browse the website, basic technical information may also be processed, including IP address, browser type, device type, referring page, pages viewed, approximate location, and timestamps. The exact information depends on the hosting, analytics, scheduling, map, and other services active on the site.

How information is used

Information may be used to respond to inquiries, schedule consultations, evaluate potential projects or applicants, provide requested services, maintain website security, understand site performance, improve content, satisfy legal obligations, and protect the rights of Fox & Timber and others.

Cookies and tracking technologies

The website may use essential storage needed for core functions and optional technologies used for preferences, analytics, or marketing. You can review and change available choices on the Your Privacy Choices page.

Third-party embeds may set or read their own cookies when loaded. Examples may include scheduling tools, maps, videos, social media, analytics, or file-upload services.

When information may be shared

We do not sell personal information for money. Information may be shared with service providers that support hosting, email, scheduling, analytics, file storage, communications, security, payment processing, or other studio operations. Information may also be disclosed when required by law, to protect legal rights, or during a business restructuring or transfer.

Retention and security

We retain information only as long as reasonably necessary for the purpose collected, contractual obligations, recordkeeping, dispute resolution, and legal compliance. We use reasonable administrative and technical measures, but no internet transmission or storage system can be guaranteed completely secure.

Your privacy choices

You may ask to review, correct, or delete personal information you have submitted, subject to legal and operational exceptions. You may also adjust available browser storage and tracking preferences through the privacy choices page. Requests can be sent to hello@foxandtimber.com.

Depending on where you live, additional privacy rights may apply. We may need to verify a request before completing it.

Children’s privacy

This website is intended for a general business audience and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 through this website.

Contact

Privacy questions may be sent to hello@foxandtimber.com or mailed to Fox & Timber, 222 West 8th St #92, Durango, CO 81301.
