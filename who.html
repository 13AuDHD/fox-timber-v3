Who we are

A company built by following the fox.

Fox & Timber grew through experimentation, adaptation, and a long trail of ideas that were often more complete in imagination than they ever became in the physical world.

The beginning

First came the ideas.

We never set out to become another design agency. Fox & Timber started with a pattern that took years to recognize. Long before there was a company… before there was a logo… and there was always another idea. A coffee company. An outdoor brand. A technology startup. A podcast.

Most people would see unfinished ideas. We saw complete worlds. We could already imagine the logo on the building… the website… the packaging… the social media… the photography… the feeling someone would have opening the box for the first time. The company existed long before anyone else could see it. Eventually we realized something important... The product wasn’t the business, but the ability to imagine the business was.

Fox & Timber became the place where that instinct stopped being dismissed as “just another idea” and became the work itself.

Why the fox

Observant. Adaptable. Resourceful.

The fox moves between environments without losing itself. That became the model for the company: flexible enough to work across industries, disciplined enough to keep a clear identity, and curious enough to find paths others overlook.
