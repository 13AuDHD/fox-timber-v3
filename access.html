Accessibility Statement

Fox & Timber wants its website and services to be usable by as many people as reasonably possible.

Our commitment

We aim to provide a website that supports keyboard navigation, readable text, meaningful structure, descriptive alternatives for important images, sufficient contrast, and compatibility with common assistive technologies. Accessibility is treated as an ongoing part of design, content, and maintenance rather than a one-time task.

Measures we take

  • Use semantic headings, labels, landmarks, and link text.
  • Provide keyboard-accessible menus, controls, dialogs, and forms.
  • Respect reduced-motion preferences where practical.
  • Use scalable text and responsive layouts.
  • Review color contrast and visible focus states.
  • Include alternative text for images that communicate meaningful information.

Known limitations

Some third-party services, embedded maps, scheduling systems, social platforms, or legacy assets may not provide the same level of accessibility as the core website. We may not control the source code or behavior of those services, but we will try to offer an alternative way to receive the same information or complete the same task when contacted.

Feedback and assistance

If you encounter a barrier, need content in another format, or need help completing an action, contact us and describe the page, feature, and assistance needed. We will make a reasonable effort to respond and provide an accessible alternative.
